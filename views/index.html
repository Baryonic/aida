Meet Aida

I'm a children's-books author and primary-school teacher with over a decade of experience sparking curiosity in young minds. Every story I write begins in the classroom, inspired by the questions, laughter, and wonder of real children.

My books blend vibrant illustrations with heartfelt stories about courage, kindness, and the joy of discovery—because every child deserves to see themselves as the hero of their own adventure.
